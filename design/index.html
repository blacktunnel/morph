Morph

This is Morph. He's a block who can jump on enemies to kill them. He's also a shape shifter. His different forms are more than just cosmetic. They also give him different abilities which can help him move around and defeat obstacles on his adventure.

Man

He can turn into a man who has the strength and arms to pick up heavy objects and throw/drop them on his opponents

Frog

He can also turn into a frog so that he's able to jump over long gaps and use his tongue as a meelee attack

Boat

As a boat, he can travel over water, and fire cannon balls at enemy ships.

Plane

In airplane mode, he can fly through the sky and shoot enemy targets. This comes in handy when morph reaches a dead end and the only way to go is up.

Jellyfish

His jellyfish form allows him to explore underwater areas.

Clock

As a clock, Morph has the ability to stop TIME. This comes in handy when surrounded by a group of enemies that move way too quickly to kill.

Flame

This is Morph as a flame (his only colorful mode). This mode allows morph to melt stuff. Lots of stuff. Enemies, trapdoors, and, of course, the terrain around him.

Monster

Morph must face many enemies along his quest. The most commonplace of which, are monsters. These suckers bite. And morph has to decide to kill them as a man with heavy objects, as a block with a good ol-fashioned jump, or as one of his many other forms. The combinations are endless.

Turret

Turrets are turrets. Gotta smash them from above.

Bird

Birds attack from above or while Morph is flying as an airplane.

Sub

Submarines are purely underwater enemies. But they're torpedos are deadly.

Robot

Robots are robots. Haven't decided what they do yet.

Boss

This is the boss. Not sure what to name him yet. He's a badass though. He can morph too. Into lots of stuff. He's also considerably bigger than Morph.

Machine

These machines are what make the game of Morph challenging. Morph can only shape shift using these machines which are placed randomly throughout the world. Choose your form wisely when you come upon one of these bad boys.
